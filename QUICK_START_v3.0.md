1. Extract `TaskMonsters-Complete-v3.0.zip`
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser

## Key Features

### 1. Focus Timer (FIXED)
The Focus Timer now displays your monster correctly as a single animated character. Set a timer (in minutes), click Start, and watch your monster keep you company while you work. When the timer completes, you'll earn Focus Power for battles!

**How to Use:**
- Navigate to the Focus Timer section
- Enter timer duration (1-180 minutes)
- Click "Start Focus Session"
- Your monster will appear and animate smoothly
- Click "Pause" or "Stop" as needed

### 3. Dialogue System (CONFIRMED WORKING)
Your monster companion talks to you throughout your journey! The dialogue system provides context-aware messages based on your actions and progress.

**When Your Monster Talks:**
- **Welcome** - 2 seconds after loading the app
- **Task Completion** - After you complete a task (category-specific)
- **Level Up** - When you reach a new level
- **Periodic Check-ins** - Every 10 minutes while idle
- **Equipment Changes** - When you equip/unequip items (if using enhanced functions)

## Troubleshooting

**Monster not appearing in Focus Timer?**
- Make sure you've selected a monster in the Monster Selection section
- Check that the timer is started (not just set)
- Refresh the page and try again

**Mood Tracker not showing?**
- Look for the tooltip above your monster on the Home tab
- Make sure you're on the Home tab
- The tooltip should appear automatically on page load

**No dialogue appearing?**
- Wait 2 seconds after loading for welcome message
- Complete a task to trigger task completion dialogue
- Wait 10 minutes for periodic dialogue
- Check browser console for any errors

**Monster animations not playing for moods?**
- If your monster is in egg form, mood animations are disabled
- Hatch your egg first by completing tasks
- Once hatched, mood animations will work
